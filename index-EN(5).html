Why Annecy?

800 m from the town centre and 15 minutes on foot from the lake — make the most of everything Annecy has to offer

🏔️

Mountains & Hiking

Semnoz, Bornes and Aravis massifs — trails for all levels just outside the city. Scenic viewpoints: Semnoz 17 km, Col de la Forclaz 12 km.

🏊

Lake Annecy

One of the purest lakes in Europe — swimming, paddleboarding, sailing, kayaking or simply strolling along its shores on foot or by bike (40 km cycle path).

🏛️

Old Town

Canals, Palais de l'Isle, castle and lively markets — a historic town centre ranked among the most beautiful in France, easily reached via the shaded Thiou riverside walk (car-free).

🚵

Sports & Activities

Cycling, skiing in winter, paragliding, via ferrata — Annecy is a European capital of outdoor sport.
